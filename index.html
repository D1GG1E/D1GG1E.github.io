My Air Force life in South Korea

My Name is Devin Snell and I live in South Korea with my wide and kids. I have been pursuing computer programming for a few years now and am excited to finish my degree next year.

My favorite hobby is carpentry. Since I was a young boy, I enjoyed creating things out of wood; Mostly boxes, and cutting boards to give away as gifts. I don't have any of my tools over here with me in Korea, so I have pivoted toward laser engraving as a hobby. This upcoming year will involve a lot of laser engraving trinkets and squadron gear to sell.

I have been studying Korean for my job for the last 13 years. I'm not sure if it's just the sunken cost fallacy talking, but I actually do enjoy it. I study it for 12-20 hours per month.

In my daily job, I deliver a lot of briefings. To get better at this, I started doing improvisational comedy while stationed in california. I still like to perform, but we don't have too many opportunities for that in Korea. I have been checking out Toastmasters lately. I think they are pretty swell.
